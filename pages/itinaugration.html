It Forum Inaugration 2024-25

On September 21, 2024, our department celebrated the inauguration of both the IT Forum and our new department website. Dr. Shreesha Bhat graced the event as the Chief Guest, with Pro Alex Ivan Sequra presiding as the Forum President. The IT Forum was launched through an innovative IoT project demonstration, underscoring our commitment to leveraging technology in meaningful ways. The department website will serve as a digital platform to keep our community informed, connected, and engaged. Together, these initiatives aim to foster collaboration, enhance learning, and spotlight advancements in information technology.
